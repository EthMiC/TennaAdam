About Us

Who we are

At Tenna-Adam home care PLC, we believe that compassionate care begins at home. Our mission is to provide high-quality, personalized nursing services that promote comfort, dignity, and independence for every client we serve.

We specialize in home-based nursing care, offering a wide range of services including post-surgical recovery, chronic illness management, elderly care, and wellness support—all delivered by licensed and experienced healthcare professionals.

Our team is committed to creating a safe and supportive environment where patients and families feel empowered and cared for. We combine medical expertise with a human touch, ensuring that every individual receives the attention and respect they deserve.

Whether you need short-term assistance or long-term care, Tenna-Adam home care is here to make home the best place for healing and living well.

Your health. Your home. Our care.

Mission

To deliver compassionate, professional, and personalized nursing care at home, ensuring comfort, dignity, and improved quality of life for every patient we serve.
